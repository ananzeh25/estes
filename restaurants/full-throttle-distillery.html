What Makes Full Throttle Special

Full Throttle operates as an actual distillery producing moonshine, whiskey, and sippin' creams on-site. Walk into the tasting room and sample as many spirits as you like - free of charge. The bartenders know the products and can recommend mixers or cocktails based on what you enjoy.

The outdoor beer garden is one of the largest patio spaces in Estes Park - spacious enough for large groups and private events. Live music plays on weekends, sports games run on multiple screens, and the atmosphere stays lively without feeling cramped. Dogs are welcome on the patio.

The service model is counter-order rather than table service. Order food at one counter, beer at the back bar, cocktails at the middle bar. It's efficient once you understand it, though first-timers may find it confusing. For traditional restaurant dining with table service and live music, The Rock Inn offers a different vibe.

What to Order (Signature Dishes)

Giant Pretzel ($19) - Massive, soft, and perfect for sharing. Comes with IPA mustard and queso. This is the most-ordered appetizer for a reason - substantial enough to split between 3-4 people.

Brisket Queso ($15) - Tender brisket over house queso with corn chips and miso salsa roja. The green chile sauce adds heat. A standout starter that works as a light meal.

Hillbilly Nachos ($14) - Fresh potato chips instead of tortilla chips, topped with pulled pork, hatch chile queso, and jalape√±os. Unique twist that works surprisingly well.

The O.G. Burger ($12) - Smash-style patties, crispy edges, still juicy inside. Well-executed for the price. Add bacon for $2.

Mac & Cheese ($15) - House cheese sauce, cavatappi pasta, breadcrumb top. Add brisket for $5 to make it a full meal. Kid-friendly option with generous portions.

üí° Pro Tip: Start at the distillery tasting room before ordering cocktails. Sample the moonshine flavors (peach, blackberry, vanilla, watermelon) to find your favorite, then order a cocktail built around it.

The Honest Take

At 4.1 stars, Full Throttle is a solid choice for the experience - not fine dining. The smash burgers, appetizers, and fried items execute well. The BBQ smokehouse items have been inconsistent (the kitchen acknowledges this and has been reworking recipes). Stick to the burgers, wings, and appetizers for the most reliable experience.

The real draw is the distillery and atmosphere. Free moonshine tastings, craft cocktails made with house spirits, a huge outdoor space, and sports games on big screens. Service can be spotty during peak times when the bars get understaffed - expect some waiting. For quick, reliable service, arrive early or on weekdays.

Who It's For (And Who It's Not For)

Best for: Sports fans wanting to watch games outdoors, groups needing space, moonshine and craft cocktail enthusiasts, families with dogs, and anyone who enjoys brewery-style casual dining with entertainment.

Skip if: You want quiet fine dining, traditional table service, or consistent BBQ - try Smokin' Dave's for dedicated smokehouse quality instead.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Full Throttle Distillery & Grill

Are the moonshine tastings really free?

Yes - walk into the distillery tasting room and sample as many house-made moonshines, whiskeys, and sippin' creams as you like at no charge. The staff explains flavor profiles and recommends cocktails based on your preferences. It's the only distillery tasting experience in Estes Park.

How much does dinner cost at Full Throttle?

Expect $20-30 per person with food and a drink or two. Appetizers run $12-19, burgers $12-20, and entrees $22-36. Cocktails are $12-16. The Giant Pretzel ($19) feeds 3-4 people, making it good value for groups.

Is Full Throttle good for watching sports?

Yes - multiple TVs throughout the indoor and outdoor spaces show games. The outdoor beer garden has heaters for cooler evenings. Sports games run Thursday through Monday. The atmosphere is lively and casual, perfect for game day.

Where do I park for Full Throttle?

Paid parking lot right next door runs about $2/hour. Free 3-hour parking is available across the street. Downtown Estes Park parking can be tricky during peak season, so the free lot is a good option when available.

Is Full Throttle dog-friendly?

Yes - dogs are welcome on the outdoor patio. The spacious beer garden has room for dogs without crowding other tables. Staff is accommodating.

How does ordering work at Full Throttle?

Counter service, not table service. Order food at one counter, beer at the back bar, cocktails at the middle bar. It can feel confusing at first but moves efficiently once you understand the system. Servers bus tables but don't take orders.

Is Full Throttle worth it?

For the distillery experience and outdoor atmosphere, absolutely. The free moonshine tastings, craft cocktails, spacious patio, and live music create something unique in Estes Park. Stick to appetizers, burgers, and fried items rather than BBQ for the most consistent food quality.
